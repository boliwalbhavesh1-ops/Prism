Public Reactions

Purpose

Summarize how people are responding to a news event using publicly available discussions.

Sources

- Reddit
- YouTube
- Public forums
- Other publicly accessible discussions, where permitted

Analysis

- Most common opinions
- Frequently asked questions
- Major concerns
- Positive and negative sentiment
- Emerging topics

Rules

- Do not display private information.
- Clearly distinguish public opinion from verified facts.
- Link to original public discussions when appropriate.

Goal

Help users understand public discourse without replacing factual reporting.
