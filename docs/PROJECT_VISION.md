Prism Project Vision

What is Prism?

Prism is a news intelligence platform that helps people understand world events through facts, evidence, context, and multiple perspectives.

Rather than showing only headlines, Prism organizes news into structured story pages that answer the most important questions about any event.

---

The Problem

Modern news is fragmented.

Readers often need to visit multiple websites to understand:

- What actually happened
- Which facts are verified
- How different countries report the event
- How different political viewpoints frame it
- What experts think
- How ordinary people are reacting
- Who is affected
- What the long-term consequences might be

This takes time and often leaves readers with incomplete or biased information.

---

Our Solution

Prism transforms every major news event into a single, comprehensive story page.

Each story includes:

- Neutral AI Summary
- Verified Facts
- Timeline
- Global Perspectives
- Political Perspectives
- Primary Sources & Evidence
- Fact Checks
- Public Reactions
- Expert Opinions
- Impact Analysis
- Related Stories

---

Long-Term Vision

Prism aims to become the world's most transparent and trusted news intelligence platform by helping readers understand information rather than simply consume it.

The platform prioritizes transparency, evidence, and context over engagement-driven algorithms.

---

Guiding Principles

1. Facts before opinions.
2. Every AI-generated insight should be traceable to supporting sources.
3. Clearly distinguish facts, claims, opinions, and predictions.
4. Show multiple perspectives without promoting any political viewpoint.
5. Promote media literacy through transparency and context.
6. Design for accessibility, speed, and trust.

---

Success Criteria

Prism succeeds when users leave with a deeper understanding of a story, not just more headlines.

Every feature should answer one question:

"Does this help someone understand the story better?"
