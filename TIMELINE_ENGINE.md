Timeline Engine

Purpose

Every major news story should automatically generate a timeline.

The timeline helps users understand how an event developed over time.

Timeline Events

- Breaking News
- Official Statements
- Government Actions
- Expert Analysis
- Fact Checks
- Court Decisions
- Policy Changes
- Final Outcome
