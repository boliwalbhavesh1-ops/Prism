Prism

«See the whole story.»

Prism is an open-source news intelligence platform designed to help people understand world events through evidence, context, and multiple perspectives.

Instead of showing just another news feed, Prism analyzes a story from different angles, helping users understand not only what happened, but also how it is being reported, who is affected, and why it matters.

Mission

To make news more transparent, understandable, and accessible by combining journalism, data, and AI.

Vision

Create a platform where every major news event can be explored from multiple perspectives, backed by reliable sources and presented with complete context.

Core Features (MVP)

- Multi-source news aggregation
- AI-generated neutral summaries
- Global perspective comparison
- Political framing comparison
- Evidence and source panel
- Story timeline
- Public reaction summaries
- Impact analysis
- Related stories
- Fast search
