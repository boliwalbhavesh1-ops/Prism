Perspectives

Purpose

Prism compares how the same event is presented from different viewpoints.

The goal is not to decide which viewpoint is correct but to help users understand differences in framing.

Perspective Categories

Global Perspective

Compare reporting from different countries.

Examples:

- India
- Japan
- United States
- United Kingdom
- China
- Australia
- Germany

Political Perspective

Where reliable public information exists, compare reporting from different editorial perspectives.

Scientific Perspective

Research findings and expert consensus.

Legal Perspective

Court decisions, laws, and regulations.

Economic Perspective

Impact on markets, businesses, employment, and trade.

Historical Perspective

Background events that provide context.

Goal

Help users understand the full picture instead of a single narrative.
