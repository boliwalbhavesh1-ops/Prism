# Prism MVP

Version: 1.0

The first public release of Prism focuses on helping users understand news through transparency and multiple perspectives.

Included Features

✅ News Aggregation

✅ Story Clustering

✅ AI Summary

✅ Timeline

✅ World Perspective

✅ Evidence Panel

✅ Search

✅ Related Stories

Not Included

❌ User Comments

❌ Browser Extension

❌ Mobile Apps

❌ AI Chatbot

❌ Personalized Feed

Goal

Launch a stable platform that solves one problem exceptionally well:
Helping people understand the full picture behind a news story.
